Current Rejection Rate

28%

Our 8-Week Mission

30%

Reduce the listing rejection rate, targeting a new benchmark below 20%.

Project Charter

Problem Statement

A high (28%) listing rejection rate is creating a "silent leak" in marketplace operations, negatively impacting agent satisfaction, customer experience, internal workload, and business liquidity. This inefficiency erodes trust and our core value proposition.

Goal & KPIs

  • Primary Goal: Reduce listing rejection rate by 30% in 8 weeks.
  • KPIs: Listing Rejection Rate (%), Avg. Time-to-Live (hours), Agent Satisfaction (CSAT), CX Ticket Volume.

Scope

In Scope

  • Redesigning agent-facing guidelines.
  • Optimizing internal review processes.
  • Targeted training (internal & external).
  • 1 week of focused engineering for fast wins.
  • Cross-functional workflow alignment.

Out of Scope

  • Major policy changes.
  • Additional budget or new hiring.
  • Long-term platform re-architecture.
  • Building separate regional solutions.

Our 8-Week Action Plan

A focused, three-track plan executable within our constraints. We will tackle process, content, and platform improvements in parallel to achieve our goal.

Track 1: Process & People

Led by Sales, CX & People

Clarify guidelines, align internal teams, and standardize agent training to ensure consistency from day one.

  1. Standardize Agent Training Deck
  2. Create Internal CX/QA Playbook
  3. Host Internal Policy Refreshers
  4. Streamline Agent Support Path

Track 2: Content & Comms

Led by Marketing, SEO & Learning

Proactively educate agents on *how* to succeed with clear, simple, and engaging content delivered through all channels.

  1. Launch "Top 5 Mistakes" Guide
  2. Create "How-To" Video Shorts
  3. Publish SEO Best Practice Guide
  4. Begin Weekly "Pro-Tip" Comms

Track 3: Platform & Tech

Led by Product, Eng & Data

Deploy high-impact, low-effort tech improvements to provide instant feedback and better data visibility.

  1. Improve In-Product Error Messages
  2. Deploy Basic Photo Validation
  3. Update QA Rejection Dashboard
  4. Analyze & Report on Initial Data

Project Milestones

Weeks 1-2: Foundation & Fast Wins

Deploy Engineering fix (Track 3). Draft all new training content & playbooks (Track 1 & 2). Establish data baseline.

Weeks 3-4: Agent Education Push

Launch "Top 5 Mistakes" campaign (Track 2). Sales teams begin rolling out new standardized training (Track 1).

Weeks 5-6: Monitor & Optimize

Data team reports on initial rejection rate impact (Track 3). CX uses new playbook and gathers agent feedback for optimization (Track 1).

Weeks 7-8: Final Review & Handover

Measure progress against 30% goal. Present final report to leadership. Handover new processes and dashboards to BAU teams.
